The Virtual Talent Contest

You may participate in multiple categories or join forces with others. After viewing each entry, we will vote on which is the best of each category.

This will consist of 3 categories:

  1. Recorded Dance Video Category - You record a Tiktok Style dance video
  2. Performance Art Category - A live musical performance, poem, drama/improv, comedy/standup routine, a roast, or a toast. If you prefer, this can be recorded in advance.

  3. One-liner Category: The seniors (50+) category. We get the best one line joke from each of the old folks. Someone will read on their behalf if they don't want to participate.

Please participate!
